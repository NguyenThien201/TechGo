    Why join TechGo

    Transport

      You are a student and spend half of your day at school? Be a part-time driver during your free time! Even when you work full-time, you can always proactively manage your schedule to take care of your family, because you yourself are your own time-manager - there is no regulation on working time or targeted income.

    High income

      Your efficiency wage could reach 24,000VNĐ/hour in case of part-time job or 10,000,000VNĐ/month in case of full-time job. There are rewarding prizes for efficient drivers weekly and monthly. Your job and your income are absolutely legal.

    Easy & Simple Sign-up

      Registration steps are simple: Fill in and submit the registration form Wait for us to contact and instruct in the document preparation process Submit the documents at our office (photocopies do not need to be notarized, but compared with the originals). You can drive more safely with Grab personal accident insurance for drivers and passengers.
